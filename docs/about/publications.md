## 📌 How to Cite

Once the DesertMIP Tier-1 forcing datasets and the official protocol paper are published, complete citation guidelines will be provided here. 

When using data from DesertMIP experiments, users will be requested to cite:
1. The primary GMD protocol paper (mentioned above).
2. The specific modeling center's dataset.
3. The general CMIP7 data citation guidelines.
