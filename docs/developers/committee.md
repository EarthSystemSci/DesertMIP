# Steering Committee

The DesertMIP framework is coordinated by an international steering committee dedicated to advancing the representation of arid environments in Earth System Models.

## Working Groups (WG)
To ensure the successful execution of DesertMIP, the committee is organized into three primary Working Groups:

1. **WG1: Forcing Data & Experimental Design:** Responsible for generating the high-resolution, transient desert fraction datasets and defining the Tier-1 protocols.
2. **WG2: Model Evaluation & Benchmarking:** Focused on assessing model outputs against satellite observations and historical proxy data.
3. **WG3: Cross-MIP Synergies:** Dedicated to coordinating joint experiments and data sharing with AerChemMIP, LMIP, and other relevant CMIP7 panels.
